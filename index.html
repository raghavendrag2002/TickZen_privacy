01

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the TickZen app for Android, developed by Lunar Eternal. We built TickZen to be a focused, private productivity tool. That philosophy extends to how we treat your data — which is to say, we don't touch it.

Plain English version: TickZen has no backend, no analytics SDK, no crash reporter, no ad network, and no login system. The app cannot send your data anywhere because it has no mechanism to do so.

02

Information We Collect

We collect nothing. Here is a full list of data TickZen stores and where it lives:

  • Your tasks — stored locally in an on-device SQLite database. Never leaves your phone.
  • Session history — focus session logs stored locally for your stats screen. Never uploaded.
  • App preferences — timer durations, sound settings, and other preferences stored in Android DataStore on your device.
  • Streak and stats data — computed entirely from your local session history. No cloud involvement.

None of this data is transmitted, backed up to our servers, or accessible to us in any way. We have no servers that receive data from the app.

03

Permissions We Request

TickZen requests the following Android permissions and only uses them for the stated purpose:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to show the persistent timer notification with pause and reset controls while a session is running. No marketing notifications are ever sent.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — to keep the timer running accurately when you switch apps or lock your screen.
  • VIBRATE — to vibrate your device when a session completes.
  • WAKE_LOCK — to ensure the timer continues in the background without being killed by battery optimisation.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — reserved for a future update that will restore an active timer after device restart.
  • INTERNET — used only to download the Space Grotesk font from Google Fonts on first launch. No user data is sent. This can be revoked after the font has been cached.

04

Third-Party Services

TickZen uses one third-party service:

  • Google Fonts (via Google Play Services) — the app downloads the Space Grotesk typeface from Google's font delivery infrastructure on first launch. This request is made by the Android system font framework, not by TickZen directly. Google's own privacy policy governs this request. No user data specific to TickZen is included in this request.

There are no analytics SDKs (no Firebase Analytics, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude). There are no crash reporting SDKs (no Crashlytics, no Sentry). There are no advertising SDKs. There are no social login SDKs.

05

Children's Privacy

TickZen is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since we collect no personal information from anyone, this applies universally — there is simply no data to collect regardless of the user's age.

06

Data Security

All data created by TickZen is stored using Android's standard app-sandboxed storage. It is protected by your device's built-in security — screen lock, encryption, and Android's permission model. We cannot access this data remotely, and neither can any third party through TickZen.

If you uninstall TickZen, all locally stored data is permanently deleted by Android. There is no cloud backup to retrieve it from.

07

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy — for example, if a future version of TickZen introduces optional cloud sync or a subscription system — we will update this page and change the "last updated" date at the top. We will also notify users via an in-app notice on the first launch after the change.

We will never introduce data collection practices that contradict the core principle of this policy without explicit user consent.
